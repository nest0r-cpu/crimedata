Crime Heat Map

This heat map visualization shows the concentration of reported crimes across San Francisco. Areas with higher concentrations of crime appear as "hot spots" in red, while areas with lower crime rates appear in cooler colors. This visualization helps identify patterns and high-risk areas in the city.

The intensity of the heat map is based on the number of reported incidents in each area. This can help law enforcement agencies optimize patrol routes and resource allocation, while also helping residents and businesses make informed decisions about safety.
