Welcome to SF Crime Analytics

As a lifelong San Francisco resident, I've watched our city's safety landscape transform dramatically over the years. The rising crime rates have not just been statistics in a report – they've been personal experiences affecting my family, friends, and community. This website is my response to the growing need for transparency and understanding of crime patterns in our city. By analyzing and visualizing crime data, we can better understand where and when crimes occur, helping both law enforcement and residents make informed decisions about safety.

This platform serves as a bridge between raw data and actionable insights. It's not just about numbers – it's about protecting our neighborhoods, supporting our local businesses, and ensuring that every resident feels safe in their community. Through this comprehensive analysis, we can identify patterns, track trends, and work together to make San Francisco a safer place for everyone.
